About Throne Holdings Group

We provide homeowners with fast, fair cash offers — without repairs, showings, or commissions.

Who Are We?

Throne Holdings Group is a direct cash home buyer. We are not real estate agents and we do not list properties. Instead, we purchase homes directly from homeowners who want a faster, simpler solution. Our goal is to offer a fair price in a quick and hassle-free manner, eliminating the stress that often comes with traditional home sales.

What We Do

We buy houses in any condition. No repairs, no cleaning, no staging, and no open houses. Whether you’re facing foreclosure, divorce, inherited property, relocation, or simply want to avoid months of uncertainty, we provide a clear and straightforward process. In many cases, we can present an offer within 24 hours and close in as little as seven days.

Why Homeowners Choose Us

It starts with a simple conversation. After evaluating your property, we present a written cash offer and allow you to choose your closing date. There are no commissions, no hidden fees, and no financing delays. Just a streamlined process designed to help you move forward quickly and confidently.
